# Pokemon and Friends Matching Game - Ultra Cool Edition

An incredibly cool and visually stunning matching game featuring Pokemon and their friends with amazing animations, particle effects, and sound!

## Features

### Visual Effects
- **Animated Gradient Background**: Beautiful, shifting gradient background that changes over time
- **Glowing Title**: Neon glow effect on the title with floating animation
- **Smooth Card Animations**: 3D card flip animations with bounce effects
- **Particle Explosions**: Colorful particles burst when you make a match
- **Victory Confetti**: Spectacular confetti celebration when you win
- **Card Hover Effects**: Cards scale up and glow when you hover over them
- **Match Glow**: Matched cards get special glowing borders

### Game Features
- **Score System**: Earn points for each match, multiplied by your combo
- **Combo System**: Build up combos by making consecutive matches
- **Move Counter**: Track how many moves you've made
- **Timer**: See how fast you can complete the game
- **Victory Screen**: Epic victory screen showing your final stats

### Sound Effects
- **Card Flip Sound**: Satisfying sound when flipping cards
- **Match Sound**: Pleasant melody when cards match
- **Wrong Match Sound**: Distinct sound when cards don't match
- **Victory Sound**: Triumphant tune when you complete the game

### UI/UX Enhancements
- **Retro Gaming Font**: Press Start 2P font for that classic game feel
- **Glass-morphism Stats**: Beautiful frosted glass effect on stat boxes
- **Responsive Design**: Works great on different screen sizes
- **Smooth Transitions**: Everything animates beautifully

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click on a card to reveal the image
3. Click on a second card to find its match
4. Build combos by matching cards consecutively for higher scores
5. If cards match, they stay face up and you earn points
6. If they don't match, they flip back and your combo resets
7. Complete all matches to see your victory screen with confetti
8. Click "Reset Game" or "Play Again" to start a new game
